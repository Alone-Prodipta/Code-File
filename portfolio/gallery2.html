    Capturing a person's essence, portrait photography is a unique art form that goes beyond a simple snapshot.
    It's about revealing character, emotion, and story in a single frame.
    Whether it's a posed studio shot with controlled lighting or a candid moment caught in natural light, a great portrait connects with the viewer on a personal level.
    The key to a compelling portrait is to build a connection with your subject.
    This means more than just telling them to "smile."
    It's about creating a comfortable and relaxed environment where their true personality can shine through.
    Focus on their eyes, as they are often the most expressive part of a person's face.
