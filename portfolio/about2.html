About Me

As a second-year college student, I am navigating life in India, discovering its unique culture and spirit through its colonies and narrow lanes.
I believe that the lifestyle of India is hidden in these neighborhoods, each with its own sounds, faces, and stories waiting to be told.

I enjoy exploring the people around me — observing their facial expressions and features,
which reflect their struggles and experiences.

For me, photography is not merely a profession but a way to freeze moments in time and capture the essence of life.
I am drawn to those who try to express themselves through small gestures, celebrations, and everyday experiences.
I especially enjoy photographing festivals and special events, where emotions and traditions come alive.

As a web developer, I view the world through logic and systems.
This perspective has enriched my hobby, allowing me to capture and document people’s lives more thoughtfully.
My sense of design — including graphic design and templates — reflects my personality and perspective.
By applying principles such as color theory, I aim to create designs that resonate with both aesthetics and functionality.

Through my portfolio, I have combined my passion for photography with my profession in web development.
I am deeply curious and always eager to learn new things.
My character is shaped by a commitment to continuous growth and self-improvement.
