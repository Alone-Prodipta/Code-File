About me!

Hi, I'm Prodipta—a curious mind navigating the space between code and candid frames. As a BSc Computer Science student, I spend my days building logical structures and solving complex problems, and my nights roaming the streets, capturing the unscripted poetry of everyday life through my lens.

Street photography is where I feel most alive—finding rhythm in chaos, emotion in anonymity, and stories in shadows. It sharpens my eye for detail and trains me to see what often goes unnoticed—skills that seamlessly complement my approach to programming and design.

This portfolio is my creative playground—a place where algorithmic thinking meets artistic intuition. Thanks for visiting; I hope you’ll see the world a little differently through my images.I’ve also built a strong foundation in languages like C, C++, Java, and JavaScript, and I specialize in crafting intuitive, responsive designs using CSS.
