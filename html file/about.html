ABOUT US!

Mainland China has become a significant name in Indian dining, recognized for its dedication to authenticity and high-quality Chinese cuisine. Over the years, the restaurant has expanded its offerings, introducing diverse regional Chinese flavors while maintaining a consistent fine-dining experience. With its carefully curated interiors and an ambiance that transports diners to China, Mainland China continues to be a favorite for food enthusiasts seeking an immersive culinary journey.

Mainland China’s menu is inspired by the culinary traditions of four major Chinese provinces—Sichuan, Guangdong (Canton), Hunan, and Shanghai. The restaurant focuses on balancing flavors through the Yin and Yang concept, ensuring a harmonious blend of spices and textures. Over the years, it has expanded across India and internationally, maintaining a reputation for authenticity, consistency, and premium dining experiences.
